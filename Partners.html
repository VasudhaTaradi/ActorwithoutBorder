Patrick Rameau

Patrick Rameau is an American Actor, Director Teacher, screen writer and playwright. His work as an actor has been seen on stage, film and television in numerous countries internationally throughout the continent of Africa, Europe, The middle east, Latin America, USA and Canada. His teaching credits include. the American Academy of Dramatic arts in New-York, Cornell University, SUNY at old Westbury Long Island, Penn State University, Acting International, The American school in Paris. among his directing credits include Athol Fugard's Sizwe bansi is dead, Statements after an arrest... The Island, Lorraine Hansbery's A raisin in the sun, Rosaries, Written and directed. Patrick Rameau has been a professional actor since 1982 and has worked extensively in Regional theaters in the US including the New York Shakespeare Festival, The Alabama Shakespeare festival, Pioneer Stage, Pennsylvania stage co. Portland stage, Mr Rameau has been seen in Avignon at the Palais des papes in Le cid As the King directed by Declan Donellan Also as Belize in Angels in America directed by Brigitte Jaques. As a Writer his play Rosaries was produced off broadway at the Riverwest Theatre directed by Carol fox prescott. and then directed by Mr. Rameau in a subsequent Off off broadway production. His current screenplay The Fall is in pre production.
